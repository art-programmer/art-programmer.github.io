I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at Washington University in St. Louis. I am advised by Professor Yasutaka Furukawa. My research interests include 3D vision and scene understanding. I am particularly interested in geometry reasoning from static images for indoor scenes.
